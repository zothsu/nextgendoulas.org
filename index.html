Birth Doula

OHP Accepted/Sliding Scale

Private Pay - 15% Retainer will go towards Next Generation Doulas and its Postpartum Access Fund

Clients will fill out a questionnaire and be matched with a THW doula who has been certified and has undergone a background check through the Oregon Health Authority.

Package: $2000

  • Two prenatal visits (in-person or Zoom)
  • Customized laminated birth plan
  • Support and community resources via phone, text, or email
  • Doula on call 24/7 from 37 weeks on.
  • Back-up doula available
  • Emotional and physical support and advocacy throughout labor and delivery
  • 8 hours of post-partum care. Two daytime visits or one overnight

Postpartum Care

FSA Accepted

At the moment, OHP is only covering 8 hours of postpartum doula care...

Private pay clients and donations will go towards providing postpartum services to OHP clients until services are covered by medicaid...

Tailored postpartum care will be provided for each family during this crucial period...

Hourly Daytime: $35 on weekdays/ $40 weekends

Hourly Overnights: $45 weekdays / $50 weekends

  • 4-6 hour day shifts
  • 8-10 hour overnight shifts
  • Assess for complications that should be followed up with a provider
  • Assess for postpartum mood disorders
  • Educate and reassure families about caring for their baby and the spectrum of typical newborn behaviors.
  • Light housekeeping
  • Meal prep
  • Lactation support & resources
  • Trauma informed care
  • Overnight infant care to let family rest
  • Sibling support
  • Referrals to community resources
